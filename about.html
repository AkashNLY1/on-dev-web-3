Our Story

Welcome to The VP Residency Towers

Welcome to The VP Residency Towers, your premier destination for luxury, comfort, and hospitality in the heart of the city. Our hotel blends modern elegance with traditional warmth, offering guests an unforgettable stay experience that goes beyond just accommodation.

Strategically located in a prime area with easy access to commercial hubs, shopping centers, temples, and transport facilities, The VP Residency Towers caters to business travelers, tourists, families, and wedding guests alike.

Our hotel is thoughtfully designed to meet the expectations of every modern guest — from leisure seekers to corporate executives. We offer a wide range of deluxe rooms, suite accommodations, multi-cuisine restaurant experiences, well-equipped banquet and conference halls, and top-tier amenities that ensure maximum comfort.

Every corner of our property reflects our commitment to quality, cleanliness, and exceptional service. At The VP Residency Towers, we take pride in delivering personalized hospitality with a smile.
